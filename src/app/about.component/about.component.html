About Makuya Civils & Projects

Our Story

Makuya Civils & Projects was founded in 2023 by Ngudo Makuya, driven by a passion for building and a desire to address unemployment in South Africa.

Although still in its early stages, the company is committed to growing carefully, delivering high-quality construction services, and creating meaningful opportunities for local talent. Our vision is to build a strong foundation that will positively impact communities as we grow.

Our Mission

To carefully grow a construction company that delivers quality projects and supports local communities through job creation.

Our Vision

To become a trusted and impactful construction company, recognized for integrity, quality, and community development as we expand.

Our Core Values

Integrity Quality Safety Innovation Teamwork
